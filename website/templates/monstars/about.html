ABOUT THE MONSTARS

Never played ultimate? Played pick up in high school? Played other sports growing up and are looking for something new to learn? Or perhaps you have never played a sport before and are simply wanting something unique and fun to join? No matter which applies to you, the University of Texas at Dallas Women's Club Ultimate Frisbee team, the Monstars, is your answer.

We are a close knit and competitive sports team that loves to work hard together, laugh together, and most importantly, be friends together. Joining the Monstars is a great way to meet new people, have a good time, and stay active and healthy, all while getting to compete and have fun.

Throughout the year we compete at home and away tournaments, usually about 3-4 per semester, with Spring semester being our in season. We practice during the week where we work on disc and field skills and athleticism. We also host several social outings and events throughout the school year along with the Men's ultimate frisbee team, WOOF.
